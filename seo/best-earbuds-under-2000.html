How to Choose Earbuds Under ₹2000

  • Sound quality: Choose balanced sound if you listen to music, movies and calls.
  • Battery life: Prefer earbuds with at least 20–30 hours total playback with case.
  • Calling: Look for ENC or noise reduction if you take frequent calls.
  • Comfort: Lightweight earbuds are better for long use.
  • Warranty: Prefer known brands with easy service support.

Who Should Buy Budget Earbuds?

Earbuds under ₹2000 are suitable for students, office users, casual listeners, gym users and people who want wireless convenience without spending too much.

What to Avoid

  • Very low-priced unknown brands with no warranty details.
  • Fake discount claims without price history.
  • Products with poor return or replacement terms.
  • Earbuds with very short battery backup.
